About Me

My name is Chad Clark. I grew up in Isanti, MN. I attended Cambridge-Isanti High School and graduated in 1996. Following that, I attended Anoka Technical College where I completed the Electrical Program. I have since been in the Electrical industry for 23 years. In that time, I have had many different roles. From having my own electrical contracting business, project manager, estimator and an electrical inspector. In my spare time, I like to spend time with my wife and two children. When I get time to do my own thing and the weather is nice, I really enjoy getting out and doing some skydiving.
